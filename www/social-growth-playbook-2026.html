Organic Tactics — What to test first

Post schedule consistency, 1–2 strong hooks in the first 3 seconds of video, micro-influencer collaborations, and hashtag A/B testing. Combine with small exchange boosts for the initial 24–72 hours to trigger discovery.

Safe Exchange Tools (Free)

Use exchange platforms that require manual engagement (points for likes/follows) rather than auto-scripts. These prioritize human actions and reduce risk of platform penalties when used sparingly.

Metrics that matter

Focus on reach, engagement rate, watch time (for videos), retention, and click-through rate. Vanity numbers (raw follower count) are secondary — aim for interaction that compounds.

Quick How-to (Safe workflow)

  1. Create your piece of content and optimize the first 3 seconds/hook.
  2. Publish at a time your audience is most active (test 2 windows for 7 days).
  3. Use a tiny exchange boost (<=5% of expected reach) to kickstart impressions.
  4. Monitor retention & engagement — iterate titles, thumbnails, and CTAs.
  5. Scale organic tactics that work—collabs, tags, and follow-up posts.
