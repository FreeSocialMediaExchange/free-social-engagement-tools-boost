How to use these tools safely (quick guide)

  • Start small: Don't flood your account — use modest redemptions to appear natural.
  • Mix with organic content: Combine exchange boosts with real posts and community engagement.
  • Avoid automation: Use manual interactions and platform-native actions where possible.
  • Respect platform rules: Read Terms of Service and avoid actions that could trigger penalties.

Tip: Use these tools to jumpstart content testing. If a post responds well, double down with organic promotion (collabs, hashtags, paid ads) rather than repeated exchange-only boosts.
