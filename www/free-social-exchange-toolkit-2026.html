How it works — 3 steps

  1. Engage: Like, follow or view other users to earn credits.
  2. Redeem: Spend credits on your target actions (followers, likes, views).
  3. Repeat & target: Focus on the platforms and posts that matter most to you.

Tip: combine small, regular exchanges with real content optimization (titles, thumbnails, hashtags) — exchange tools help visibility but content keeps people coming back.
