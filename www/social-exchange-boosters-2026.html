How these exchanges work — quick explainer

  2. Spend credits: redeem credits to receive followers, likes, views, or subscribers.

Safe-usage tips

  • Don’t overuse for accounts linked to sensitive services or business-critical profiles.
  • Mix exchange-driven traffic with organic posts, paid ads, and influencer outreach.
