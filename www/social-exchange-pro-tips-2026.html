Safety & Best Practices

Tip 1 — Avoid automation

Don't connect bots or automation tools. Manual or platform-native actions keep accounts compliant with most TOS.

Tip 2 — Pace your growth

Spread redemptions over days or weeks to mimic natural growth and reduce the chance of platform penalties.

Tip 3 — Use for testing

These tools are great for testing headlines, thumbnails, or creative variants—use them to get early signals.

Tip 4 — Quality over quantity

Focus on engagement (comments, saves, watch time) rather than raw follower counts for long-term success.
